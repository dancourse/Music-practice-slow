Ways to use PracticeLoop from anywhere on the web

🔖 Bookmarklet Free

One click to open any YouTube video in PracticeLoop. Drag the button below to your bookmarks bar, then click it whenever you're on a YouTube video you want to practise.

▶ Practice This

Drag this button to your bookmarks bar

1

Drag to your bookmarks bar

Click and drag the purple "Practice This" button to your browser's bookmarks bar.

2

Find a YouTube video to practise

Go to YouTube and find any tutorial, performance, or lesson you want to learn from.

3

Click "Practice This"

Click the bookmarklet in your bookmarks bar. PracticeLoop opens in a new tab with the video pre-loaded, ready for speed control and AB looping.

On mobile? Copy the link manually

Bookmarklets don't work well on mobile. Instead, copy a YouTube video URL, then paste it into PracticeLoop.

🔗 Share a Practice Link

Already set up a loop in PracticeLoop? Copy the URL from your browser and share it. Recipients get the same video, loop points, and speed pre-configured.

1

Set up your practice session

Load a video, set AB loop points, adjust speed.

2

Click "Copy Link" in the share section

PracticeLoop generates a URL with video ID, start/end times, and speed encoded.

3

Send to students, bandmates, or friends

They open the link and get the exact same practice session. No signup needed.
