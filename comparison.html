Why Progressive Speed Training Matters

Every music teacher will tell you: start slow, master the passage, then gradually increase the speed. But manually adjusting the tempo after each loop rep is tedious and breaks your flow.

PracticeLoop automates this workflow. Set your starting speed (e.g. 0.5x), target speed (1.0x), and how many reps before bumping up (default: 3). Then just practice. The tool automatically increases the speed when you're ready, with visual progress and celebration when you hit your target.

No other YouTube looper offers this. It's the difference between a tool built for musicians and a tool built for generic video looping.
